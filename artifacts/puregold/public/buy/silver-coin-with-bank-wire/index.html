Frequently Asked Questions

Are American Silver Eagles eligible for a Gold IRA?

Yes. American Silver Eagles (any year 1986–present) are IRS-approved for self-directed IRAs. They must be purchased from an IRS-approved dealer and stored at an approved depository.

What is the difference between a roll and a tube of silver coins?

A roll typically refers to 20 American Silver Eagles in a mint-sealed tube. A tube of Canadian Maple Leafs contains 25 coins. Both are sealed at the government mint.

Can I buy silver coins from other countries?

Yes. GoldBuller carries UK Britannia coins, Austrian Philharmonics, South African Krugerrands (silver), and Australian Kangaroos. All are .999 or .9999 fine and IRA-eligible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to have gold delivered to my home?

Yes, when done correctly. GoldBuller uses plain outer packaging with no precious metals markings, full declared-value insurance through our carrier policy, and mandatory signature on delivery. Never leave gold shipments unattended at your door.

Will my neighbors know I received gold?

No. The outer shipping box has no indication of the contents. The return address shows a neutral commercial address from our Dallas fulfillment center. Inner packaging is sealed and opaque.

What happens if my gold is lost or damaged in shipping?

All GoldBuller shipments are insured for 100% of declared value. In the event of confirmed loss or damage, GoldBuller files the claim and replaces or refunds your order.

Do you ship to PO boxes?

No. UPS and FedEx do not deliver to PO boxes, and signature-required delivery is not possible. All gold must ship to a physical street address.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are American Gold Eagles 24 karat gold?

No. American Gold Eagles are 22 karat (91.67% fine gold) with the remainder being silver and copper for hardness. However, a 1 oz Gold Eagle contains exactly 1 troy ounce of fine gold — identical fine gold content to a 24K bar.

Are American Gold Eagles IRA-eligible?

Yes. American Gold Eagles are specifically approved for inclusion in self-directed precious metals IRAs under IRS Publication 590-A. They must be stored at an IRS-approved depository — not at home.

What is the premium on American Gold Eagles vs. spot?

At GoldBuller, 1 oz American Gold Eagles typically carry a 5–8% premium over spot. Wire payment eliminates the additional 3.5% card surcharge, saving ~$120 per coin vs. credit card at $3,500/oz gold.

Can I buy fractional American Gold Eagles (1/10 oz) by wire?

Yes. GoldBuller accepts wire for all Eagle sizes. The 1/10 oz Eagle (~$250–$300 each) is popular for gifting. A minimum wire order of $500 covers approximately 2 fractional coins.

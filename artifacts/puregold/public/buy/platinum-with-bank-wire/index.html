Why is platinum cheaper than gold right now?

Platinum's price is primarily driven by automotive catalyst demand and industrial applications. The global shift away from diesel vehicles has suppressed demand. Historically, platinum traded at a premium to gold.

Are American Platinum Eagles IRA-eligible?

Yes. American Platinum Eagles (.9995 fine) are IRS-approved for precious metals IRAs and must be stored at an approved depository.

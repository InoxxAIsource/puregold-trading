Frequently Asked Questions

How heavy is a 100 oz silver bar?

A 100 troy ounce silver bar weighs exactly 100 troy ounces = 6.857 lbs = 3.11 kg. It is roughly the size of a large TV remote control. Plan your storage: 10 bars = ~68 lbs.

Are 100 oz silver bars IRA-eligible?

Yes, if they are .999 fine or better. All GoldBuller 100 oz silver bars meet this standard. For IRA purchases, specify an approved depository at checkout.

Is there a discount for buying multiple 100 oz bars by wire?

Yes. Wire orders of 10+ bars (1,000+ oz) qualify for bulk pricing — typically an additional $0.10–$0.25 per oz reduction. Contact our OTC desk for exact pricing on orders over $30,000.
